FAQ

What is
Sharwa.Finance?
Sharwa.Finance is an advanced trading protocol offering margin trading with up to 10x leverage, as well as sophisticated option-based products.
What sets Sharwa.Finance apart?
  • We pride ourselves on offering a top-tier UI/UX specifically tailored for traders.
  • Users have the ability to purchase option strategies with just one click, leveraging liquidity from multiple option markets, and have the flexibility to pay with any asset.
  • For strategies that carry limited risk, users aren't required to provide collateral for short options. This is due to Sharwa.Finance's unique cross-margin feature tailored for specific option strategies.
  • Our integrated margin system for both options and margin trading facilitates the use of options as hedging tools, minimizing the risk of liquidations. Sharwa.Finance stands out as the pioneer trading protocol that accepts option positions as collateral.
  • We offer Stop-Orders for option positions and Uniswap, all powered by the reliable Chainlink technology.
Where does the liquidity come from?
Sharwa.Finance doesn't act as the counterparty for traders. Instead, we route all orders to other protocols. For options trading, we utilize Hegic, Lyra, and Premia. For margin trading, we rely on Uniswap.
What is the economic model of Sharwa.Finance?
  • When using Margin Trading and No-Collateral One-Click Strategies, users are charged an interest fee on borrowed capital.
  • For the execution of stop-orders, users are only responsible for covering gas fees; no additional charges apply.
I'm not a trader. How can I benefit from Sharwa.Finance?
Even if you're not actively trading, you can still contribute capital to our liquidity pools and earn attractive APY returns. Click here to explore this opportunity and get started.
Does Sharwa.Finance have its own token?
No, Sharwa.Finance does not have a token at this time.
